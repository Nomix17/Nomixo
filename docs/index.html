Features

Has everything you need.

A lean desktop app built on Electron. No bloat, no tracking, no upsells.

Browse & search

Home page with popular content, real-time search across movies, shows, and anime, and a discovery page by genre.

Hardware-accelerated playback

Streams via WebTorrent and plays through MPV with full hardware acceleration. Saves your position automatically.

Download queue

Full download manager with pause, resume, cancel, and reorder support.

Library & history

Save favorites, track watch progress, and resume exactly where you left off.

Auto subtitles

Auto-download via Wyzie, or load local .srt / .vtt files. Configure font, size, color, and opacity.

Fully themeable

Customize colors and the entire appearance through Settings. Your setup, your look.

How it works

Simple by design.

Under the hood it's a smart pipeline. From your side, it's just pick something and watch.

1

Search or browse

Nomixo pulls metadata from TMDB. Search anything or browse popular movies, series, and anime organized by genre.

2

Pick your stream

Nomixo fetches a magnet link from Torrentio and starts buffering it with WebTorrent over a local HTTP stream.

3

MPV takes over

The app launches MPV pointed at that stream. The Nomixo window steps aside while you watch and reappears when you're done.

4

Progress saved automatically

Playback position is written to disk. Resume from where you stopped.
